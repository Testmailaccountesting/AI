Employees are entitled to 2 paid holidays per month.
Overtime hours are paid at 1.5x the normal rate.
Employees must submit leave requests at least 3 days in advance.
Personal laptops must be secured with company-approved passwords.
Confidential information must not be shared outside the company.
Internet usage for non-work purposes is limited to 30 minutes per day.
Employees are required to attend mandatory monthly team meetings.
Casual Fridays are allowed, but clothing must remain professional.
Smoking is prohibited within company premises.
Company-provided devices must be returned upon termination.
Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
Employees must complete annual compliance training modules.
Expense reimbursements must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.
Remote work requires manager approval and adherence to security protocols.
Employees must clock in and out using the official time-tracking system.
